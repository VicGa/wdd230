Site Plan

Site Description

In this website the public will be able to find out about the weather in the most important cities of the world and where they live, they will also be able to find out about news about the weather, such as earthquakes, sunamis and other catastrophes. As well as the security measures to act in states of emergency.

Site Name

This site is going to be called WhatWonderfulWeather.com. As the name indicate, It will be able to find out about the weather.

Purpose Statement

The primary purpose of WhatWonderfulWeather.com is to help people learn about the weather in the world's major cities and where they currently live.

The second purpose is to help people get news about the weather.
